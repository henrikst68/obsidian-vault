# 🗓️ Collaboration Morning Brief — Wednesday, 22 July 2026

## 🔵 Microsoft Teams
- Microsoft is rolling out **Organization Trust Score** for apps/agents plus a new **Pro Management portal** for admins, adding automated trust evaluation, unsent-draft access, and simplified external collaboration controls in the Teams Admin Center.Microsoft Teams adds Organization Trust Score for apps and agents, easier access to unsent drafts, and simplified controls for external collaboration settings in Teams Admin Center. Microsoft Teams adds Pro Management portal controls for device health, inventory and analytics, lets users report suspicious external users, centralizes Teams Core agent management.
- Rollout of **apps in private channels** (bots, message extensions, tabs) is nearly complete, expected fully live by end of July, though a related "minimized meeting" reaction/raise-hand update has been **paused** for bug fixes.Microsoft says it has almost finished rolling out support for Apps in private channels, but if you still don't have it, try checking again by the end of July 2026. Microsoft has paused the rollout of a new minimized meeting update... Some users disliked the approach, and Microsoft decided to pause the rollout, as it needed more time to patch the bugs.
- New chat organisation is coming: dedicated **"Muted"** and **"Meeting"** chat sections, replacing existing meeting chat filters, alongside invites now sent from the actual inviter's email rather than no-reply addresses.Microsoft Teams can send invites using the inviter's email address, so you will no longer have no-reply emails used for guest invitations. Microsoft is creating two new chat sections: one called Muted and another called Meeting.
- Admins should note July's release notes on **Teams Phone call transfer improvements** and **sensitivity label inheritance** for meeting recordings — labelled recordings (e.g. "Confidential") will now carry the same protections as the source meeting.Microsoft's official "Release notes for Microsoft Teams" page... the latest July 2026 entry highlights Teams Phone Call Transfer Improvements plus sensitivity label inheritance for meeting recordings. Microsoft says Teams meeting recordings can now automatically inherit the meeting's sensitivity label when admins enable label inheritance.

## 🤖 Copilot & AI in Teams
- **Copilot Cowork**, Microsoft's agentic "plan-execute-deliver" system, is now **generally available worldwide**, returning completed deliverables rather than drafts.Copilot Cowork, an agentic system that plans, executes, and delivers real work, is now generally available worldwide. Users define the task, and Cowork executes it from start to finish, returning a completed deliverable rather than a draft or recommendation.
- A watchdog report (July 21) found **no confirmed platform-wide Copilot outage**, though scattered sign-in loops and loading issues persist across surfaces — worth flagging to IT if users report problems.Microsoft Copilot does not appear to be suffering a confirmed, platform-wide outage as of July 21, 2026, at approximately 18:50 UTC, although individual users and organizations may still encounter blank windows, endless loading, sign-in loops.
- Microsoft is reportedly leaning more on its own **MAI models** for Copilot to improve efficiency alongside existing Anthropic/OpenAI partnerships, with "Auto" mode letting Microsoft swap models transparently.Bloomberg reports Microsoft is using more of its own MAI models in M365 Copilot scenarios — more efficient on non-consumption workloads, and adding model resilience alongside Anthropic and OpenAI. It's also why "Auto" is the default in so many Copilot scenarios.

## 🔗 Ecosystem & Integrations
- Fresh from InfoComm, Microsoft unveiled a **Teams Phone Agent** (AI auto-attendant tied to Copilot Studio), multi-line calling GA, and a mobile Queues app GA — a significant push into AI-driven telephony.Teams Phone — multi-line, mobile Queues, call screening. Teams Phone Agent — AI auto attendant + Copilot Studio.
- **Viva Learning Academies** are coming to Teams mobile (view-only), letting employees access curated learning paths on the go while admins retain full control via web/desktop.Academies in Viva Learning will be available in Microsoft Teams mobile, letting learners access organisation-curated learning journeys directly from their devices. Academies on mobile will be view-only. All creation, permissions and curation will continue to be managed on Viva Learning web and desktop.
- Partner **Neat** has shipped an **MCP server** for its device management platform — the first OEM tooling vendor to do so — plus an intelligent camera-framing beta for larger rooms.Neat have recently announced a couple of big things: Neat Pulse MCP Server — manage devices, rooms and sensor data in plain language (beta). Lots of people have been talking about MCP and API connectivity to OEM tooling, and Neat are first out of the gate with it.

## Meetingroom Equipment
- Channel research (Blue Touch Paper) shows **Logitech, Cisco, Neat, Yealink, Q-SYS and Shure** gaining momentum in the Teams Rooms channel, while **HP Poly, Crestron, Barco, Jabra and EPOS** are losing ground.The Blue Touch Paper report names Logitech, Cisco, Neat, Yealink, Q-SYS, and Shure as the vendors gaining channel momentum. Conversely, HP Poly, Crestron, Barco, Jabra, and EPOS are losing ground.
- Reasons cited: growing vendors align tightly with Teams and scale deployment easily, while decliners struggle with slower innovation and weaker native Teams fit.The growing vendors align tightly with Teams, deploy easily at scale, and carry strong commercial terms. The declining vendors struggle with slower innovation and weaker native fit with the Teams ecosystem.
- Certified hardware and management is consolidating around Microsoft's **Pro Management Portal**, with Android and Windows Teams Rooms devices now unified under one console across manufacturers.Management of Android-based Teams Rooms, Teams panels, and Teams phones is moving from the Teams admin center to the Pro Management portal, providing a consistent and secure management experience across devices and manufacturers.
