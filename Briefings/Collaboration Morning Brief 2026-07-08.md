# 🗓️ Collaboration Morning Brief — Wednesday, 8 July 2026

## 🔵 Microsoft Teams
- **Partial outage confusion (July 7):** Microsoft Teams suffered a partial outage on July 7 that went undetected by many monitoring services, with UptimeRobot flagging elevated response times while other trackers and Microsoft's own Service Health Dashboard showed no incident. Worth flagging to your IT team as a reminder to diversify monitoring signals.
- **Teams Rooms admin upgrades:** New releases add BYOD peripheral health reporting, restored conversation context, tighter meeting bot admission, CSV breakout room assignment, and live captions profanity filtering now off by default to align with EU accessibility requirements.
- **Room builder tool:** The Teams room builder in the Pro Management portal is a visually engaging tool helping IT managers design and configure Teams Rooms spaces, enabling faster design, standards creation, and simplified device/license purchasing.
- **Private channels upgrade:** Teams is moving private channels to a shared mailbox model so compliance policies apply consistently, while raising the private channel limit beyond 30 up to 1,000 total channels per team.

## 🤖 Copilot & AI in Teams
- **Cowork governance matures:** Purview controls now extend to Copilot Cowork, helping organizations manage security and compliance with the same tools used for Microsoft 365 Copilot, with this reaching general availability in July.
- **Model choice expands:** Claude Opus 4.7 is coming to Microsoft 365 Copilot, helping complete complex, multi-step work with stronger instruction following and higher-resolution visual understanding.
- **Licensing shift:** From July 1, Microsoft 365 Business Standard and Premium with Copilot become permanent SKUs rather than promotional add-ons, giving SMB customers an always-on path to AI adoption — relevant if Henrik manages SMB licensing conversations.

## 🔗 Ecosystem & Integrations
- **Copilot Notebooks + Outlook:** Microsoft is extending Copilot grounding so users can add Outlook emails as reference sources in Copilot Notebooks, letting Copilot draw on conversations and decisions held in email, rolling out through July.
- **Pricing changes live:** As of July 1, Microsoft's 2026 update includes significant investments in Defender, Intune, Security Copilot, and collaboration features tied to the new global M365 pricing.
- **Viva Engage in Teams:** Viva Engage communities are now available directly in Teams alongside teams, chats, and channels, letting users connect and share knowledge without leaving Teams.

## Meetingroom Equipment
- **Logitech AI push:** At recent InfoComm showcases, Logitech highlighted the Rally Camera AI & Rally Camera AI Pro, advanced AI-powered dual-camera systems featuring RightSight 2 for large meeting rooms.
- **Vendor momentum shifting:** Channel data shows Logitech, Cisco, Neat, Yealink, Q-SYS, and Shure gaining channel momentum, while HP Poly, Crestron, Barco, Jabra, and EPOS are losing ground among Teams Rooms resellers.
- **New device platform on horizon:** A new Microsoft framework called the Microsoft Device Ecosystem Platform (MDEP) is about to change which devices resellers recommend — worth watching for procurement roadmaps.

## 🌐 Broader Collaboration Landscape
- **Slack becomes CRM default:** Since June 15, every new Salesforce Enterprise and Unlimited org ships with a fully provisioned Slack workspace as the default collaboration layer, replacing Chatter — a notable competitive shift for Teams in CRM-adjacent workflows.
- **Zoom expands cross-platform AI:** Zoom's AI Companion now lets users take notes in meetings beyond Zoom itself, working across third-party platforms like Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and WebEx.
- **Google Workspace steady cadence:** Google Workspace continues weekly feature rollouts, with its release notes last updated 2026-07-06, underscoring Google's rapid iteration pace versus Microsoft's monthly cycle.

## ⚡ Action Items & Things to Watch
- Confirm with IT whether the **July 7 Teams service degradation** caused any real user impact in your org, and check Service Health Dashboard subscriptions are current given the monitoring blind spot.
- Review licensing/renewal timing — the **July 1 M365 price increase** and new permanent Copilot SKUs may affect budget conversations this quarter.
- Watch the rollout of **Outlook-as-reference-source in Copilot Notebooks** and **MDEP** device certification changes — both could shape Teams/Copilot adoption planning over the next month.
