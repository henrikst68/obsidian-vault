# 🗓️ Collaboration Morning Brief — Friday, 15 May 2026

## 🔵 Microsoft Teams

- **Chat organisation overhaul rolling out in May 2026:** Muted chats will move to their own dedicated section by default, and meeting chats get an optional separate section (disabled by default). A new eye-icon toggle lets users flip between unread and all messages without multiple clicks.
- **⚠️ Teams on the web browser deadline is TODAY (15 May):** Microsoft is now enforcing ES2022-compliant browsers for Teams web. Users on outdated browsers will hit a blocking page — admins should verify end-user browser versions immediately.
- **March 2026 GA highlights (now in production):** Multi-account/multi-tenant activity feed consolidation, automatic EXIF metadata stripping from shared images, a redesigned AI-powered Workflows app (no-code Copilot automations), and a Drafts quick-view for unsent messages.
- **CVE-2026-32185 — Teams spoofing vulnerability (disclosed 12 May):** Affects Teams for Android; CVSS 5.5 / Important severity. No privileges required to exploit. Patch available on the Google Play Store (build 1.0.0.2026092103). No known active exploitation yet.

## 🤖 Copilot & AI in Teams

- **Copilot Cowork goes mobile (5 May):** Cowork — Microsoft's task-delegation layer built on Work IQ — is now available on iOS and Android, allowing users to hand off work on the go and return to completed outcomes. It also gains reusable **Skills** (saved workflow templates) and **Plugins** (native connectors to Fabric/Power BI, Dynamics 365, and soon LSEG, Miro, monday.com, S&P Global).
- **GPT 5.5 Instant now in Microsoft 365 Copilot:** Low-latency model optimised for common work queries, image-based inputs, and STEM tasks — speeds up everyday Copilot interactions.
- **Planner integration in Copilot Chat:** Users can now create tasks, check due dates, and view team assignments directly inside Copilot Chat without switching apps. Copilot in Teams Phone is also fully unified with M365 Copilot Chat, enabling context-aware real-time call summaries drawing on emails, files, and meeting history.

## 🔗 Ecosystem & Integrations

- **Microsoft 365 E7 (Frontier Suite) + Agent 365 went GA on 1 May:** E7 bundles E5 + Entra Suite + M365 Copilot + Agent 365 into a single SKU, with Agent 365 acting as the unified governance and orchestration plane for enterprise agents across the organisation.
- **5,000+ AI solutions on Microsoft Marketplace:** As enterprises move AI from pilots to production, the marketplace is becoming the primary procurement channel — partner-built agents are increasingly searchable and deployable from there.
- **Altigen + Tollring compliance recording partnership:** Altigen will distribute Tollring's Analytics 365 (call recording + AI analytics) to Teams Operator Connect and Direct Routing providers in North America — relevant for compliance-heavy verticals.

## 🖥️ Meeting Room Equipment

- **New Teams-certified devices (March 2026 wave):** Yealink MP55 E2 (Android 15 / MDEP, AI noise cancellation, high call-volume desk phone), Huddly C1 videobar (on-device AI director with 20× more AI processing, for small/medium rooms), and two Owl Labs + HP Poly bundles (Meeting Owl 3 and Owl 4+ with 4K video).
- **Neat Center 360° companion device certified:** Designed to work alongside existing Neat devices, it captures in-room participants who are far from the main unit or facing inward — improving hybrid equity in larger spaces.
- **Google Meet ↔ Teams Rooms two-way interoperability (Direct Guest Join):** Teams Rooms on Windows can now join Google Meet meetings, and vice versa. A meaningful step for multi-vendor hybrid environments.

## 🌐 Broader Collaboration Landscape

- **Zoom Workplace continues expanding its platform:** Zoom AI Companion (meeting summaries, action item extraction, smart recordings) is now included at no extra cost on all paid plans — matching what competitors charge for as premium. Zoom is clearly repositioning beyond video meetings.
- **Slack and Webex incremental updates:** Slack has improved AI transcription and task automation features; Webex has enhanced its noise removal for cleaner audio in variable environments. Neither announced major platform-level news this week.
- **AI features are now table-stakes across the market:** In 2026, real-time transcription, auto-generated meeting summaries, and action item extraction are baseline expectations at every price tier — the competitive battleground has shifted to agentic and cross-app workflow capabilities.

## ⚡ Action Items & Things to Watch

- **🚨 TODAY — Verify browser compliance for Teams web users:** The ES2022 browser enforcement is live as of today. Check with IT that no users are running legacy browsers; affected users will be blocked outright.
- **Patch Teams for Android ASAP:** Push the CVE-2026-32185 update (Play Store) to all managed Android endpoints. Prioritise regulated or high-security environments.
- **Consider joining the Microsoft Frontier Program:** Copilot Cowork (Skills, Plugins, mobile) is currently Frontier-only. If your organisation is evaluating agentic AI workflows, this is the fastest path to hands-on access.
